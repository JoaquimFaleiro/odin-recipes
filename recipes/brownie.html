Brownie

Description

The brownie is a versatile and delicious dessert that's perfect for any occasion.
Whether you're craving a quick snack, an afternoon treat, or a grand finale to a special meal, a brownie is always a good choice.

Ingredients

  • 6 heaped tablespoons of unsalted margarine
  • 1/2 cup (tea) cocoa powder
  • 2 cups (tea) sugar
  • 2 pinches of salt
  • 1 bar of semisweet chocolate, chopped into cubes
  • 3/4 cup (tea) chocolate powder
  • 1 and 1/4 cup (tea) wheat flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract or essence
  • 1/2 cup (tea) of chopped walnuts or granulated cashew nuts

Steps

  1. Mix the eggs and sugar.
  2. Then add all the other ingredients until you get a uniform cream.
  3. Pour into a baking tray lined with baking paper and bake in a medium oven for 40 minutes.
  4. The brownie is ready when the top is lightly browned and, when you insert a toothpick , it is slightly moist (due to the melted chocolate).
  5. Cut into squares while still warm and serve with a scoop of vanilla ice cream, or freeze in a freezer bag.
  6. To defrost, place the brownie on a dessert plate and heat in the microwave, on high power, for 1 minute.
